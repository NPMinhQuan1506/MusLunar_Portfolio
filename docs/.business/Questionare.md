# What do I want in this product?

**Goal:** Help me interrogate my needs and goals from every angle.

---

# 1. Identifying Needs

### WH-Questions
- **What is the problem or situation I am facing?**  
  There is no place to show my skills, showcase projects, or connect with customers and recruiters.
- **What do I really need or want in this context?**  
  I want a website that:  
  - Tells my story and wins admiration (personal brand).  
  - Impresses recruiters so I can land a dream job (target: 100M VND).  
  - Convinces customers to hire me (freelancer target).
- **Why am I interested in this problem/issue?**  
  It is required for my career goals and freelancing path.
- **Who else is involved or affected?**  
  Me, and my family if the portfolio is missing or weak.

### Yes/No Questions
- **Is this need genuine or just temporary?** Real and foundational for personal branding.
- **Do I fully understand what I need?** Yes: a unique portfolio (3D/AI/multiverse style) that shows both me and my wife, adapts to visitors, and highlights uniqueness immediately with strong UI/UX/performance.
- **Can I clearly state my need in one sentence?**  
  I want a site that tells the story of my (dev) and my wife's (UI/UX) skills, products, and journey in a unique, modern style.

---

# 2. Goals & Expectations

### WH-Questions
- **What is my ultimate goal or outcome?** Attract viewers (customers, recruiters) and show who we are and what we have.
- **Why is this goal important to me?** It impacts income and opportunities; income is a tool for long-term happiness.
- **How will I measure success?** Viewers, shares, interactions.
- **When do I expect to achieve this goal?** MVP 31/12/2025 (kick-off 04/12/2025); full feature 27/03/2026; admin 30/04/2026.
- **Who will notice if I succeed?** Family, community, customers, recruiters.

### Yes/No Questions
- **Is my goal specific and realistic?** Yes; some features are challenging but manageable.
- **Do I have clear expectations of the outcome?** Yes:  
  - Happy path: >85% features, impress customers, first client worth ~20M VND.  
  - Worst path: delays, drop impractical features, or poor traction.
- **Will achieving this goal make me happy/satisfied?** Yes; it boosts confidence and opportunities.

---

# 3. Deeper Motivation & Core Values

### WH-Questions
- **Why do I truly want to achieve this goal?** Prove myself, build brand, get dream job, attract clients, foundation for future business.
- **What value or meaning does this bring?** More income opportunities, progress toward goals, higher confidence.
- **How does this align with my values?** Strong alignment.
- **Who inspires me?** Family.

### Yes/No Questions
- **Is this aligned with my core values?** Yes.
- **Do I feel strong internal motivation (vs external pressure)?** Mixed, but I will commit to finish.

# 4. Commitment & Resources

### WH-Questions
- **How much time/energy/resources can I commit?** ~9h/week: 9-10PM Mon-Fri, 12-2PM Sat/Sun; 3M VND/year budget.
- **What skills or support do I need?** AI support; designer partner.
- **Where can I find the resources?** Freelance/small jobs.
- **When can I start, and how to stay consistent?** Start 04/12/2025; penalty 50K VND per missed day.

### Yes/No Questions
- **Can I realistically commit?** Yes.
- **Do I have enough resources/support?** ~60% confidence.
- **Will I overcome obstacles?** Likely yes.

---

# 5. Long-term Impact

### WH-Questions
- **How will achieving this affect my future?** Builds foundation for future products.
- **What will I do after reaching this goal?** Phase 2: update content, projects, background.
- **Which areas of my life will this impact?** Some personal time impact.
- **Who else benefits long term?** Family and partner.

### Yes/No Questions
- **Is this goal sustainable over time?** Yes.
- **Will it open new opportunities?** Yes: jobs and income streams.
- **Do long-term benefits outweigh costs/risks?** Yes; costs 3-5M/year and 9h/week (~432h) with opportunity cost 60-86M.

---

## 1. Summary of Costs and Risks

### Actual Costs
- Hosting/domain/services: 3-5M VND/year.
- Time: 9h/week x 48 weeks = 432h/year; opportunity cost ~60-86M VND.
- Total per year: cash 3-5M; time-valued 60-86M; combined ~63-91M VND.

### Risks
- Time not fully used; portfolio unseen; no clients/jobs.
- Portfolio not attractive or market-fit; fails to convert.
- Strong competition; need ongoing updates.
- Investment may not pay off without clear direction.

---

## 2. Long-term Benefits

### Higher chance of landing a good job
- Personalized portfolio with real projects and product mindset stands out; improves screening and interview chances; supports higher salary.

### More freelance/remote opportunities
- Portfolio required for big projects (Upwork, LinkedIn, Behance, GitHub); can yield 1-2 side jobs/year.

### Personal brand and career growth
- Builds industry impression and network; can lead to training, mentoring, consulting, lead roles; may evolve into products/services.

### Skill development
- Improves UI/UX, writing, SEO, DevOps, PM, CI/CD, deployment, cloud.

### Control and negotiation power
- Inbound interest lets you lead the game and negotiate value with confidence.

---

## 3. Cost-Benefit Analysis

### Average success
- 1 small freelance job: ~10M.
- Better full-time job: +3M/month = 36M/year.
- Intangible brand/learning upside.
- Minimum benefit after 1 year: ~46M (cash only).

### Outstanding success
- 2-3 freelance jobs: 30-60M/year.
- Job hop or promotion: +60-100M/year.
- Brand/network value: high upside.

### Failure scenario
- No jobs; only skills gained.
- Loss: 3-5M + opportunity cost.
- Experience can be reused next year.

---

## 4. Conclusion
- Long-term benefits clearly outweigh costs/risks.
- Even without full payback in year one, accumulated brand/skills/network repay over time.
- Breaking even or profiting in year one is realistic with focus.
